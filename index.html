About Me

Student and Software Engineer

My name is Trevor Jex, and I graduated from American Fork High School on May 31, 2018. I am currently studying Computer Science at Brigham Young University. I have a passion for learning, creating and designing.

My favorite languages are Python and JavaScript. I love web design, especially when it comes to CSS or websockets, and I love Linux as well as Android.

My hobbies include playing the piano, playing tennis and frisbee, listening to new-age music, and designing innovative apps and programs.

I currently work as part of an agile development team for Brigham Young University Broadcasting. Our work includes the websites and apps for BYUtv and BYU Radio.
